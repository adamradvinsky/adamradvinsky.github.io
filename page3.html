My Favourite Music












































My favourite music is probably game music. I'm not really into rap, classical, or very specific types of music. I don’t go out of my way to follow popular music artists. My way of listening to music is to just randomly stumble upon a song. It may be playing in the background of a YouTube video and I may look it up. It can be the opening song to a show I really enjoy, or the background music of a video game that I play nonstop. A great example of this is one of my favourite songs, Hornets theme by Christopher Larkin. This song is from the popular Metroid-Venia hollow knight and is played when you are fighting a specific boss. So, when I fought the boss, the music started to play, and I really enjoyed it so I now listen to it nonstop. I would have never found this song if it wasn’t in the background of my game. So, because of this weird way I listen to music, I don’t have a real preference.
